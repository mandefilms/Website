M&E Films is a husband and wife film team that leverages modern video production tools to create powerful stories that move audiences in entirely new ways. Our process extends beyond the studio; we are a highly capable field production team that excels in capturing cinematic moments through non-intrusive methods, blending immersive visuals and sound to help our clients connect with their audiences, moving them to action.

Every story demands it's own approach.

We produce films in multiple forms: donor-driven shorts that drive action, character-focused narratives that empower, documentary interviews capturing unfiltered perspectives, and immersive projects that place audiences inside the story. Some are nuanced. Some are bold. All are intentional, designed to inform, shift perspectives, and amplify voices that need to be heard.
