FAQ

We don’t want “AI chaos.” Will this disrupt our existing pipeline?

No. The audit is specifically designed to keep what already works, remove friction, and only introduce tools where they measurably reduce time or rework.

Do you build custom models?

Usually not for this stage. Most teams get strong ROI from tooling + workflow design. If a custom build becomes necessary, we’ll recommend it transparently.

We’re early to AI - is that okay?

Perfect. We’ll help you avoid the common trap of adding tools without process. You’ll end with a simple stack your team can actually maintain.

What do you need from us to start?

A short intake form, one stakeholder call, and (optionally) a walkthrough of a recent project so we can see where time is really going.
