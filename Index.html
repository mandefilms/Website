Who We Are

Mande Films is a husband-and-wife film team that leverages modern production tools to create powerful stories that move audiences in entirely new ways. We are a highly capable field production team that excels in capturing cinematic moments through non-intrusive methods — blending immersive visuals and sound to help our clients connect with their audiences and move them to action.

Our Approach

Every story demands its own approach.

We produce films in multiple forms: donor-driven shorts that drive action, character-focused narratives that empower, documentary interviews capturing unfiltered perspectives, and immersive projects that place audiences inside the story. Some are nuanced. Some are bold. All are intentional — designed to inform, shift perspectives, and amplify voices that need to be heard.

Latest Work

We're always exploring the newest tools as our industry constantly evolves — ensuring our clients continue to stand out. We've recently introduced a 360 camera, FPV drone, and the latest AI tools, all running locally. This adds to our creative arsenal while challenging us to keep growing as filmmakers and storytellers.
