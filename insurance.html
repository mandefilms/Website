About This Service

Northfield Assets provides routine independent property documentation — weekly timestamped, geotagged imagery that creates a verifiable baseline of normal conditions across your portfolio. When something happens, the before is already documented.

How It Helps
01

Support faster claims

Before and after comparison is immediate. No disputes about pre-existing conditions, no delays waiting for evidence.

02

Demonstrate proactive management

A consistent documentation history shows properties are actively monitored — reducing risk profile and supporting underwriting decisions.

03

Catch issues before they become claims

Small problems flagged early rarely become expensive ones. Weekly visibility means nothing festers unseen.

The Bottom Line

Not a replacement for formal inspections. An independent evidence base you can rely on when it matters most.
