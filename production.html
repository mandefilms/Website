How We Work

How We Work

We operate as a field production team supporting marketing agencies, production companies, and in-house teams.

We work from:

  • Concise briefs and creative decks
  • Predefined shot lists
  • Interview questions provided in advance
  • Live remote direction via Zoom, iPad, or similar

Our focus is execution and risk reduction.

What We Deliver on Set

We are accustomed to working under time constraints, with non-technical clients present, and with remote producers monitoring live.

  • Three-camera 4K interview capture
  • Professional audio and controlled lighting
  • Structured b-roll aligned to agency requirements
  • Calm, efficient on-set presence
  • Organized media delivery ready for post

Single and multi-day production. Footage delivered on drives or uploaded same day.
